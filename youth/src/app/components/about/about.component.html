Khanya-Nathi Youth Co-Op

Khanya-Nathi Co-Op is a cooperative based in a small town called Lusikisiki under the deep rural areas of eQaukeni in Mgojweni location (Eastern Cape). It is directed by 3 personnel, Lwazikazi Macingwane, Yanga Faku, Siphesihle Tansi. The purpose of our cooperative is to release the economic, cultural and social needs of the community members and other surrounding communities.

We produce crops such as spinach, cabbage and beetroot. We have supplied different communities with our crops icluding one of the largest supermarkets in South Africa, Boxer Superstore. Our purpose is to create dependency and employment amongst the youth as there is an increasing rate of unemployment and promoting the culture of "Vukuzenzele Darkie".
